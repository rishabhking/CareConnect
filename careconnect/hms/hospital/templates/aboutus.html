Abouts Us

The Health Center at IIIT Allahabad is a dedicated facility committed to providing comprehensive healthcare services to students, faculty, and staff. Equipped with experienced medical professionals and essential diagnostic tools, the center ensures timely care and promotes overall well-being. Regular health check-ups, emergency medical support, and wellness programs are integral parts of its offerings. With a focus on creating a healthy campus environment, the Health Center fosters physical and mental health, ensuring that the IIITA community thrives in a supportive and safe atmosphere.
